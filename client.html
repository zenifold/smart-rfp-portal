Understanding SMART's Challenges

We've listened. We understand your pain points.

Data Friction

Your teams work harder than necessary. Manual data re-entry, disconnected systems, and inefficient workflows waste valuable time and introduce errors.

User Resistance

Previous software implementations failed because they prioritized technology over people. Low adoption, workarounds, and hidden "shadow processes" are the norm.

Scope Creep

Budget uncertainty plagues ERP projects. "Consultant bleed" through hourly billing, unclear deliverables, and shifting requirements has left you wary.

FTA Compliance

As a transit agency, you face unique regulatory requirements. Grant reporting, NTD submissions, and DBE compliance can't be an afterthought.

Our Approach

Not just another consultant. A transformation partner.

Corporate Ethnography

We don't just interview your staff. We observe them in context. We watch dispatchers coordinate routes, maintenance technicians track parts, and finance teams reconcile expenses.

This reveals the "Shadow ERP" - the hidden workarounds and informal processes that actually keep SMART running. These aren't problems to solve; they're opportunities to understand how your organization truly functions.

Our Methodology

Four pillars that ensure success

01

Service Blueprints

Visual maps showing user actions, front-stage touchpoints, back-stage processes, and pain points. Standard consultants provide requirements lists; we provide actionable insights.

  • User journey mapping
  • Front-stage/back-stage analysis
  • Pain point identification
02

Data Hygiene First

We institute "Pre-Migration Data Hygiene" - cleaning vendor master files and asset lists BEFORE the vendor is allowed to import them. No "garbage in, garbage out."

  • Data quality assessment
  • Data cleansing rules
  • Three-phase migration
03

Integration Handshake Testing

We don't just check if data moves between Trapeze and ADP. We test if it moves CORRECTLY when things go wrong. API reliability under failure conditions.

  • Positive path testing
  • Negative path testing
  • Failure recovery
04

Guardian Oversight

During vendor execution, we stand between SMART and the software vendor. We protect your interests, prevent scope creep, and ensure deliverables meet requirements.

  • Independent oversight
  • Scope change management
  • Quality assurance
