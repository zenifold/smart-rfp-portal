# Grant Compliance Matrix
## FTA Requirements Mapping to RFP 26-4364 Proposal

**Purpose:** This document maps Federal Transit Administration (FTA) grant requirements to our proposed deliverables, ensuring SMART maintains compliance throughout ERP System Upgrade project.

---

## Overview

As a recipient of FTA grants, SMART must ensure all ERP-related activities align with FTA Circular requirements. My methodology and deliverables are designed to facilitate compliance tracking, reporting, and audit readiness.

---

## FTA Circular Requirements Mapping

### 1. FTA Circular 5010.1D: Grants Management

| Requirement | Our Deliverable | Compliance Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **5.1 Financial Management System** - Grantees must maintain financial management systems that track grant funds | ERP Financial Module Implementation | Financial module design includes: <br>• Grant code tracking<br>• Allowable vs. disallowable cost categorization<br>• Grant fund balance monitoring<br>• FTA-required report templates |
| **5.2 Cost Allocation** - Costs must be allocated to specific grant projects | Workforce Management Module | Time and attendance system with: <br>• Project/grant code assignment per employee<br>• Automated cost allocation rules<br>• Labor distribution reports by grant<br>• Audit trail for cost transfers |
| **5.3 Match Contributions** - Non-federal share tracking | Financial & Procurement Modules | Match contribution tracking: <br>• Cash vs. in-kind match recording<br>• Match percentage calculation<br>• Match fund source documentation<br>• FTA-required match reporting |
| **5.4 Budgetary Control** - Obligation tracking and budget monitoring | Financial Module & Implementation Partnership | Budget controls: <br>• Grant obligation tracking<br>• Budget vs. actual variance reports<br>• Automated alerts when approaching limits<br>• Period-to-period budget rollover tracking |

### 2. FTA Circular 4220.1F: Third Party Contracting Requirements

| Requirement | Our Deliverable | Compliance Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **6.1 DBE Goal Achievement** - Meet DBE participation goals | Building Better Communities, LLC (DBE-Certified) | Our approach: <br>• BBC, LLC as DBE-certified entity<br>• DBE subcontracting opportunities for ERP vendors<br>• Good faith efforts documentation<br>• DBE participation tracking and reporting |
| **6.2 DBE Good Faith Efforts** - Document outreach if goals not met | Implementation Partnership & Vendor RFP | I ensure: <br>• DBE participation requirements in ERP vendor RFP<br>• Documentation of DBE outreach efforts<br>• Evaluation criteria weighting for DBE participation<br>• Good faith effort documentation if goals not met |
| **6.3 Contract Monitoring** - Monitor subcontractor DBE commitments | Implementation Partnership Model | I monitor: <br>• Vendor DBE subcontractor performance<br>• DBE payment tracking<br>• Subcontractor compliance with FTA requirements<br>• Regular DBE status reporting to SMART |

### 3. FTA Circular 5010.1D: Procurement Requirements

| Requirement | Our Deliverable | Compliance Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **4.1 Procurement Methods** - Competitive bidding for contracts > $250,000 | ERP RFP Development & Vendor Evaluation | My deliverables: <br>• FTA-compliant procurement process design<br>• Competitive solicitation documentation<br>• Bid evaluation methodology<br>• Award justification documentation |
| **4.2 Vendor Qualification** - Assess contractor capability | Vendor Evaluation Framework | Evaluation criteria include: <br>• FTA compliance history<br>• Past performance on FTA-funded projects<br>• Financial capacity assessment<br>• DBE commitment history |
| **4.3 Cost Principles** - Ensure allowability of costs | Implementation Partnership During Implementation | I review: <br>• Vendor cost proposals against FTA cost principles<br>• Reasonableness of labor rates<br>• Allowability of indirect costs<br>• Consistency with FTA Circular 5010.1D |

### 4. FTA Circular 4702.1: Uniform Guidance

| Requirement | Our Deliverable | Compliance Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **§200.75 Allowable Costs** - Costs must be reasonable and allowable | Financial Module Design & Implementation Partnership | System enforces: <br>• Cost categorization (allowable vs. disallowable)<br>• Reasonableness checks on vendor proposals<br>• Documentation requirements for unusual costs<br>• FTA-required cost principle codes |
| **§200.82 Standards for Financial Management Systems** - Internal controls | Financial Module Implementation | Internal controls include: <br>• Separation of duties (user permissions)<br>• Automated approval workflows<br>• Transaction audit trails<br>• Reconciliation processes for grant funds |
| **§200.86 Property Standards** - Asset tracking | Asset Management Module | Asset tracking capabilities: <br>• Unique asset identification<br>• Acquisition cost tracking<br>• Use/maintenance records<br>• Disposal documentation (FTA requirements) |

### 5. National Transit Database (NTD) Reporting Requirements

| Requirement | Our Deliverable | Compliance Mechanism |
|-------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| **Monthly Data Submission** - Operating, financial, safety data | Data Architecture & Integration Design | My approach: <br>• NTD data element mapping to ERP fields<br>• Automated NTD report generation<br>• Data validation rules (FTA NTD specifications)<br>• Pre-submission error checking |
| **Data Quality Standards** - Ensure data accuracy and completeness | Quantitative Data Analysis Methodology | Data quality controls: <br>• Pre-migration data cleansing rules<br>• Validation checks during data entry<br>• Required field enforcement<br>• Data completeness reports with metrics |
| **Audit Trail** - Track changes to submitted data | Financial & Operations Modules | Audit capabilities: <br>• Complete change history for NTD data<br>• User attribution for all changes<br>• Before/after value tracking<br>• NTD revision history maintenance |

## Compliance Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Vendor FTA Non-Compliance
**Mitigation:** Implementation Partnership Model
- Independent testing of all FTA-required features
- Vendor deliverables validated against FTA requirements before acceptance
- Warranty clauses for compliance deficiencies

### Risk 2: Data Quality Issues Affecting NTD Reporting
**Mitigation:** Quantitative Data Analysis Methodology
- Pre-migration data cleansing with FTA NTD specifications
- Validation rules embedded in ERP system
- Regular data quality monitoring with metrics during implementation

### Risk 3: DBE Goals Not Met
**Mitigation:** DBE-Certified Entity & Good Faith Efforts
- Building Better Communities, LLC as DBE-certified entity
- DBE participation requirements in ERP vendor RFP
- Documentation of outreach efforts if goals not met
- Implementation Partner monitoring of DBE subcontractor performance

### Risk 4: Grant Fund Misallocation
**Mitigation:** Financial Module Design & Implementation Partnership
- Grant code mandatory field on all transactions
- Automated cost allocation rules
- Real-time grant fund balance monitoring
- My review of cost allocation methods

### Risk 5: Audit Findings
**Mitigation:** Audit Trail & Documentation
- Complete transaction audit trail in ERP system
- FTA-required documentation templates built into workflows
- Pre-audit readiness review
- My support during FTA audits
